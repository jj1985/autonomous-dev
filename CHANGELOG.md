- **Cumulative prompt drift threshold calibrated from 15% to 30%** (Issue #870): `MAX_CUMULATIVE_SHRINKAGE` in `prompt_integrity.py` is raised from `0.15` to `0.30` (30%) to reduce false positives caused by normal inter-issue prompt variance.
